Calliope

Muse of Epic Poetry

the Muse of epic poetry and eloquence, known as the chief of the nine Muses. She inspires heroic and grand tales like Homer's Iliad and Odyssey.
In this card, I aim to depict the start of a hero's journey.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

Chariot, Fool, Hierophant.

This set of cards suggests you're heading into a new adventure with both excitement and control, while still being open to learning from tradition or mentors. It’s a balance of boldness and wisdom—take risks, but don’t be afraid to seek guidance along the way.


"And here Calliope, strike a higher key,
Accompanying my song with that sweet air"

Thalia & Melpomene

Muses of Comedy & Tragedy

Together, they represent the duality of theatrical art—comedy and tragedy—capturing both the joyful and melancholic aspects of human experience.
In this card, I design to show the constant change and shift in drama, depicting lovers, devil and justice.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

Lovers, Devil, Justice.

There might be a choice to make regarding the question, urging you to consider whether it aligns with your values and brings balance. The message could be to confront any toxic elements with clarity and seek a resolution that promotes growth and fairness for both parties. Overall, it encourages a careful assessment of love and commitment while being mindful of potential pitfalls.


"The world is a comedy to those that think; a tragedy to those that feel."

Urania

Muse of Astronomy

Often depicted with a globe and a compass, she inspires the study of the stars and the cosmos.
In this card, stars and moons are drawn to show her role as the muse of Astronomy.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

Sun, Moon, Stars.

This combination reflects a journey from clarity and success of hope and inspiration . It may suggest that while you are experiencing joy and fulfillment in some aspects of your life, there may be underlying fears or confusion that you need to confront. Embracing your intuition can help you navigate these uncertainties, ultimately leading to a renewed sense of hope and direction.


"...but the sight of the stars makes me dream."

Polyhymnia

Muse of Geometry and Meditation

She inspires devotion and spiritual expression.
Though she is mostly recognized as the muse for hymns, her role in geometry can't be ignored.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

Tower, Hermit, Wheel of Fortune.

This set encourages you to accept the chaos of change, take the time to reflect on its significance, and trust that the cycle of life will bring new beginnings. The key message is to find wisdom in upheaval and remain open to the possibilities that follow.


"Geometry is knowledge of the eternally existent."

Euterpe

Muse of Music

She embodies the joy and inspiration that music brings to life. Her name means "giver of delight".
Double flute is her symbol, I combined it with the the Judgement element in Tarot.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

The Magician, Judgement

This duo suggests you have the tools and resources to shape your future. Together, they call for embracing personal power, making confident decisions, and taking action to bring about meaningful change, using both self-awareness and skill to guide your path forward.


"Where words leave off, music begins."

Erato

Muse of lyric and erotic poetry

Her name means "beloved," reflecting her role in capturing the essence of love and the emotions that accompany it.
With a theme of love, I give her wings, symbolizing her connection with Eros.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

Strength, Death

This card encourages you to face transitions with courage and acceptance. Embrace the idea that endings are often necessary for new beginnings and that your inner strength will guide you through the transformative journey ahead. This spread reminds you that resilience and compassion can be powerful allies during times of change.


"A Being breathing thoughtful breath,
A Traveller between life and death;"

Terpsichore

Muse of Dance

She encourages artists to celebrate the beauty and emotion conveyed through physical movement.
I depicted her in the form of reflection on water, dynamic and moving.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

The Hanged Man, Temperance

These two cards together suggest that you're in a situation where things might feel stuck or like you're in limbo, but the key to moving forward is finding balance and patience. It’s encouraging you to see things from a new perspective and trust the process, blending the highs and lows of the moment to find peace.


"I was the world in which I walked, and what I saw
Or heard or felt came not but from myself"

Clio

Muse of History

Clio represent the preservation of knowledge through time. Her name means "to celebrate" or "to make famous"
In this card, she is playing chess-a mirror of real-world conflicts, power struggles, and social dynamics.

————————————————————————————————

Tarot element:

Emperor, Empress, The High Priestess

This tarot spread suggests a dynamic interplay between authority, nurturing, and intuition. It encourages you to lead with confidence while remaining open to the deeper, more subtle insights that come from within. The combination of these cards emphasizes the importance of both structure and compassion in achieving your goals.


"History is written by the victors."
